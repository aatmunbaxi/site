Computing

I’ve spent a lot of time developing a workflow that works well for me, mainly centered around my time typesetting math, and finding better ways to do so. Here I record some things that I’ve learned along the way.

Emacs org-mode for LaTeX

About four years ago, my brother introduced me to Linux and free and open source software. Around the same time I learned to use LaTeX to typeset documents with lots of mathematical notation. I then stumbled across Gilles Castel’s article on typing LaTeX with vim, and I was amazed. For those who don’t know, typing LaTeX by default is a terrible experience. Terrible. Using a program like vim with plugins made typing LaTeX so frictionless that I immediately dove into the world of extensible text editors....
